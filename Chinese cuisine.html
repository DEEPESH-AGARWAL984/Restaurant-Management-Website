Introducing new varieties of Chinese Dishes

Veg Dry Manchurian

"These amazingly delicious vegetarian Chinese balls loaded with veggies will perfectly cater to all your snack cravings and leave you wanting for more. This quick and easy dish made with cabbage, carrot, spring onion, capsicum, ginger, garlic, corn flour, maida, can be enjoyed with fried rice and noodles."

East-West Spring Rolls

"Infused with a range of flavours, crisp vegetarian spring rolls are stuffed with cheese,bell peppers and sundried tomatoes. It is served with a basil dip made with olives, parmesan, mayonnaise and a hint of garlic."

Veg Hakka Noodles

"You can have it for a light brunch or a party. The recipe is a hit with children too! Toss up your favourite veggies like capsicum, carrot, spring onions and cabbage in the eclectic mix of sauces and you have a winner! "

Manchow Soup

"An authentic Chinese fare without an ever-so delightful soup? Cant happen. Wont happen. Savour the hot and spicy flavours of this Chinese vegetable manchow soup that wouldn't take much of your time."

Honey Chili Potato

"Honey chili potato can be added to any party food menu as veg Chinese starter and is a sure-shot paradise for potato lovers. Made with fried potatoes, honey and chili sauce, this snack is crunchy, flavourful and irresistible."

Crunchy Iceberg Dumpling

Pamper your taste buds with this phenomenal iceberg dumpling recipe, packing the fresh and juicy goodness of asparagus, crunchy water chestnut and corn kernels these dumplings are sure to leave an everlasting impact.
