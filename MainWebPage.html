North Indian Cuisine

North Indian curries usually have thick, moderately spicy and creamy gravies. The use of dried fruits and nuts is fairly common even in everyday foods.

South Indian Cusisine

South Indian cuisine is perhaps the hottest of all Indian food. Meals are centered around rice or rice-based dishes. Rice is combined with Sambaar and rasam.

Chinese Cuisine

Chinese cuisine is an important part of Chinese culture and includes cuisines originating from China. Chinese cuisine has influenced many other cuisines in Asia.
